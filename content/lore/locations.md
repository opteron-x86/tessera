# Tessera — Locations

*Companion to [world.md](world.md). This file tracks major regions, settlements, ruins, rifts, and travel hazards.*

---

## Continents and Regions

**Merata** — The central continent and primary setting. Its Spellbreak scars include rifts, dead zones, unstable magic, Aberrations, and ruins from a larger pre-catastrophe world.

**Arlengh** — The northern continent, sealed since the Spellbreak behind permanent storm and ice. It was once home to human peoples and giants. No reliable expedition has returned, and the fate of its survivors is unknown.

**Isenfall** — Northern tundra and taiga of Merata. Its settled peoples descend from Arlengh migrants who crossed before the Spellbreak sealed the north. Older indigenous tribes survive only in diminished fragments.

**Teutania** — Northwestern highlands between Isenfall and the human heartland. Martial, insular, and effectively independent because the terrain is hard to conquer.

**Pritani Jungle** — Southeastern jungle homeland of the Reshai. Dense, ancient, and largely unmapped by outsiders.

**Sahridi Desert** — Spellbreak-made desert over buried Vahari ruins. The Sehrati live there and treat the exposed ruins as sacred ground.

**The Blightlands** — Interior region permanently damaged by the Spellbreak. Rifts, unstable magic, and Aberrations are common.

**Gloamwood** — Altered forest bordering Spellbreak damage. Light, navigation, and animal life behave strangely. Were-creatures and wandering Aberrations are associated with it.

**The Orathian Sea** — Sea around the Shattered Isles. Home waters of the Myrathi and the occasional route of Sea Giant hulks.

## Cities and Polities

**Cassidor** — Merathine imperial capital at the fertile delta of the Maren River.

**Tindaire** — College and library city.

**Lorindale** — Prosperous human kingdom in the fertile heartland south of the Maren (see [Geopolitics](geopolitics.md)).

**Seradon** — Southern coastal kingdom on the Orathian Sea and the Meratan coast most exposed to Myrathi activity (see [Geopolitics](geopolitics.md)).

**Vrasar** — Rugged northeastern human kingdom where Merathine reach gives way to the frontier facing the Banner-Sworn (see [Geopolitics](geopolitics.md)).

**The Vezhan Steppe** — Eastern grasslands beyond Vrasar's marches. Its horse clans have united under a single Khal as the Banner-Sworn (see [Geopolitics](geopolitics.md)).

**Juna** — Northern kingdom between the Dyrathi mountains and Isenfall.

**Natari** — Mostly intact pre-Spellbreak Kynathi city on the southern coast. Capital of the Natari Dominion and the living center of Kynathi tradition.

**The Shattered Isles** — Archipelago at the center of Myrathi civilization.

**Neresce** — Alnathi necropolis, its true citizens are the dead kept in terraced vaults; the living Vigil, lay servitors, pilgrims, and petitioners are custodians.

The Vigil maintains the **Reading of the Rolls**, a continuous recitation of the harbored dead. The uncopyable Vigil Original of the **Nerescine Codex** remains here, consulted only under rite.

## Ruins and Anomalous Sites

**Lethengarde** — Deserted northeastern city that once operated a great observatory.

**Illuvinel** — Southwestern pre-Spellbreak ruin. Once a city of towers, sky bridges, tiered plazas, lifts, waterworks, and hanging gardens; now collapsed and overgrown.

**Arhebel** — Underground ruin of vaults and catacombs.

**Isrannaes** — Mountainside ruin with sealed chambers that may have been a refuge, instrument, vault, or some other purpose.

**Loneridge** — Frontier settlement around a contained Spellbreak rift. It began as a mining town, became a ghost town, and is now an unlikely boomtown.
