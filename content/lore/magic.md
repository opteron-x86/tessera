## Magic

Before the Spellbreak, magic was a structured discipline — part of the underlying framework through which the Creators shaped and sustained Tessera. Practitioners worked within a reliable system, and the Kynathi in particular had developed it to extraordinary sophistication.

After the Spellbreak, the framework cracked. Magic still functions but it is less predictable, less consistent, and occasionally wrong. Most magical practice in the current age is safe and functional. Wild magic events are rare and tend to cluster near rifts or sites of significant Spellbreak damage.

One thread runs beneath every people's practice: blood. In Tessera, blood is the living medium of the weave. This is why blood oaths genuinely bind, why bloodline claims carry more than ceremony, why vampires are what they are, and why the practices mortals call blood magic are less a school than a temptation.
