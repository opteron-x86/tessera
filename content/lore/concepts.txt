Three thousand years ago, the Spellbreak shattered the hidden framework through which the Creators shaped and sustained the world. The catastrophe severed their connection to creation, changed the laws of magic, weakened the boundaries between planes, and left portions of reality permanently wounded. No one knows whether the Spellbreak was caused by mortal ambition, divine war, a failed ascension, the weaponization of creation itself, or the influence of something outside reality. All that remains are ruins, contradictory histories, unstable magic, silent gods, and a world that continues to function only because fragments of the old design still hold.

Beyond mortal reality lies a dead astral expanse called the Graveland that is inhabited by powers that never became true Creators, or whose creations failed and died. They are divine intelligences without living worlds, severed from the mortal connection that gives divinity purpose and form. For ages they watched the created worlds from beyond the sealed framework of reality, unable to enter, unable to create, and unable to change. The Spellbreak damaged that framework. Now, through rifts, dreams, broken leylines, and reckless magic, these failed gods can reach into the world. Mortals call them demons, devils, false gods, angels, saints, or the real gods returned. The Kynathi called them the Daeva. None of those names are quite right. The beings themselves may no longer remember what they were. They only know that the world is alive, and they are not.

Daeva include (incomplete list): Leraye, Chayliel, Iphareth, Syrath, Mahadron, Razael, Nymeris, Vaurun

Kynathi are High Elves, talented magic users, were at the height of their power pre-Spellbreak, many venerate the ancestor-gods they call the Avara. Long lived and slow breeding.

Alnathi are Dark Elves, which share some common traits with the Kynathi, but have dark gray/blue/purple skin tones and dark hair, Daeva worship is common and not considered evil in traditional Alnathi culture.

Myrathi are Sea Elves, most different from Kynathi and Alnathi, feared by all sailors, they occupy a large archipelago in the Orath Sea. Seeing or hearing their impossibly fast warships is a death knell to all. Sometimes allied with Sea Giants.

Humans are diverse and exist all over the continent of Merata.

Giants are a race in decline in Merata, mainly keeping to their own and avoiding the more prolific races, except when defending their territory. Sometimes they require tribute or offering depending on the location and their relationship with nearby human or other peoples.

Sea Giants are terrible and feared Giants that live on massive sea hulk ships, of which there are 3 known to exist. They are rarely seen, but spotting the titanic ships on the horizon is a harbinger of doom, they are known to launch attack craft against other ships and will make landfall in port towns to pillage and capture slaves to put to work on the ship.

Reshai are a monkey-like people that call the Pritani Jungle home. Mostly keeping to themselves and practicing their own spiritual and divine veneration not common to other races.

The Graveland is home to myriad creatures and entities not native to the world of Tessera. These can sometimes enter the world via rifts or summoned by wizards.
